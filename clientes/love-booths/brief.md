## SERVICE DESCRIPTION

Love Booths provides a dedicated phone setup at events — weddings, quinceañeras, and similar celebrations — where guests record personalized video messages for the host. The recorded videos are sent directly to the event host after the event.

**How it works:**
1. Love Booths sets up a dedicated phone station at the event venue
2. Guests approach the booth and record a personal video message
3. All videos are compiled and delivered directly to the event host (bride, quinceañera, etc.)
4. The host receives a permanent collection of heartfelt memories from every guest who participated

**What's included in the rental:**
- Dedicated phone / recording device (provided and configured by Love Booths)
- Setup and breakdown at the venue
- Video collection and delivery to the host

## VALUE PROPOSITION

> Love Booths turns your guests' words into a forever memory — without asking anyone to remember to record.

**Core promise:** Every guest gets the chance to leave a personal video message. The host receives all of them in one place, ready to watch forever.

**What makes it different from regular video booths or photo booths:**
- Video messages are personal and emotional — not posed photos
- No reliance on guests' personal phones (quality, format, sharing issues)
- Centralized delivery: one collection, not scattered across 50 phones
- Guests who are camera-shy at a photo booth will open up for a private video message
- Works as a structured activity that keeps guests engaged during cocktail hour or downtime

---

## COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

| Competitor type | What they offer | Love Booths advantage |
|---|---|---|
| Traditional photo booth | Printed/digital photos | Video messages are more emotional and personal |
| Guest app (e.g., WedPics) | Guests upload their own photos/videos | No action required from guests beyond recording; curated, not scattered |
| Professional videographer | Full event video | Love Booths captures what a videographer misses: what guests actually wanted to say |
| DIY (guests record on their phones) | Uncoordinated, inconsistent | Dedicated device, one delivery, no chasing guests for files |

**Key differentiator to lead with in marketing:**
> "The one thing your wedding video won't capture — what your guests actually wanted to say to you."
