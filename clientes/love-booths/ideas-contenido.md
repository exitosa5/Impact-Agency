## EMOTIONAL / STORYTELLING (High-reach, high-save)

### 1. 🎬 "The message she didn't expect"
**Hook:** "She thought the wedding was over. Then she saw the videos."  
**Concept:** Show a bride watching the video messages for the first time — genuine reactions, tears, laughter. No voiceover needed. Let the emotion carry it.  
**CTA:** "Your guests have something to say. Make sure you hear it. 🔗 Link in bio."  
**Production note:** Film the reveal moment with permission. Face and screen visible. Golden hour or candlelit lighting preferred.

---

### 2. 🎬 "What your wedding video won't show you"
**Hook:** "Your photographer captures the dress. Your videographer captures the dance. But nobody captures *this*."  
**Concept:** Split screen or sequential clips — professional wedding shots vs. raw, emotional video messages from guests. Contrast the polished vs. the personal.  
**CTA:** "The part of your wedding you'll rewatch the most."  
**Production note:** Use real event clips with permission. B-roll of professional wedding video on one side, Love Booths messages on the other.

---

### 3. 🎬 "Grandma's message"
**Hook:** "Her grandmother couldn't dance. But she had something to say."  
**Concept:** Story of an elderly guest recording a heartfelt message — slow movement, emotional, genuine. No dancing, no big moments — just love in words.  
**CTA:** "Give every guest a voice. 💛"  
**Production note:** Recreate or use real footage (permission required). This is the highest-emotion piece in the library. Treat it carefully.

---

### 4. 🎬 "The quinceañera she'll never forget"
**Hook:** "She turned 15 once. These are the words she'll read at 30, 40, 50."  
**Concept:** Montage of different guests — friends, cousins, grandparents — recording messages at a quinceañera. End with the quinceañera watching them.  
**CTA:** "Love Booths — memories that outlast the party."

---

### 5. 🖼️ "Words vs. photos" (carousel)
**Hook slide:** "Your guests took 300 photos at your wedding. How many of them said what they really felt?"  
**Content:** Each slide shows a written excerpt from a real guest message (anonymized or recreated) — funny, emotional, heartfelt. Final slide: "Imagine having all of these. Forever."  
**CTA:** "Book Love Booths for your event."  
**Note:** Use real message excerpts with permission, or write realistic examples.

---

## HOW IT WORKS / EDUCATIONAL (High-save, objection-removal)

### 6. 🎬 "How Love Booths works in 30 seconds"
**Hook:** "Ever wished your guests left you more than a gift? Here's how."  
**Concept:** Clean, fast walkthrough — booth setup → guest records → host receives videos. No clutter, just clarity.  
**CTA:** "Simple. Personal. Forever. DM us to book."  
**Production note:** Use text overlays for each step. Upbeat background music.

---

### 7. 🖼️ "5 reasons Love Booths isn't a photo booth" (carousel)
**Hook slide:** "It looks like a photo booth. It's nothing like a photo booth."  
**Slides:**
1. Photos capture looks. Videos capture voices.
2. Photos fade. Words stay.
3. A photo booth needs props. Love Booths needs just your guests.
4. You scroll through photos. You *watch* video messages.
5. A photo booth gives you a strip of paper. Love Booths gives you a collection of love.
**CTA:** "The difference is everything. 🔗 Book yours."

---

### 8. 📲 "What happens to the videos?" (Story series)
**Concept:** FAQ story sequence — tap through to answer the top questions guests and hosts have.  
- Slide 1: "We get asked this a lot 👇"
- Slide 2: "Do the videos stay private?" → Yes. Only you receive them.
- Slide 3: "What format do I get them in?" → [answer]
- Slide 4: "How long does setup take?" → [answer]
- Slide 5: "How do I book?" → DM us / link in bio
**Note:** Use poll stickers or question stickers to boost engagement.

---

### 9. 🎬 "Setup to delivery — what you never see"
**Hook:** "The setup took 20 minutes. The memories will last forever."  
**Concept:** Behind-the-scenes time lapse of Love Booths setup at a real venue, then fast-forward to guests using it, ending with the host receiving the video file.  
**CTA:** "You worry about the dress. We handle the memories."

---

### 10. 🖼️ "Which events work best with Love Booths?" (carousel)
**Hook slide:** "Not just weddings. Love Booths works for all of these."  
**Slides:** One per event type — wedding, quinceañera, baby shower, graduation, anniversary — with a one-line reason why it works for each.  
**CTA:** "Planning one of these? Let's talk."

## SOCIAL PROOF / TESTIMONIALS (Trust-building)

### 11. 🎬 "She booked it thinking it was a nice touch. It became her favorite part."
**Hook:** "She almost didn't add it. Now she says it was the best decision of the whole wedding."  
**Concept:** Testimonial-style reel — bride (or mother) speaking directly to camera about the experience. Real, unscripted, authentic.  
**CTA:** "Hear it from the people who've lived it."  
**Production note:** Interview format, no heavy production. Authenticity > polish here.

---

### 12. 📸 Review highlight — real quote over event photo
**Concept:** Beautiful wedding or quinceañera photo as background. Overlay a real client review quote (with permission). Clean, emotional, shareable.  
**Example quote:** *"I've watched the videos 12 times since the wedding. I cry every single time."*  
**CTA:** "Your guests have more to say than you think. 💛"

---

### 13. 🎬 "We asked 5 brides what surprised them most"
**Hook:** "We asked 5 brides the same question. None of them gave the same answer."  
**Concept:** Quick cuts — different brides answering "What surprised you most about Love Booths?" Authentic, varied, relatable.  
**CTA:** "Curious? Book yours and find out."

---

### 14. 📲 Story — screenshot of real message from client
**Concept:** Share a real WhatsApp or DM message from a client post-event (with permission and name removed). Something like: *"We watched all the videos last night. We laughed, we cried, we couldn't stop. THANK YOU."*  
**Caption:** "This is why we do this. 💛"

## TRENDING / REACH-FOCUSED

### 15. 🎬 "POV: You're a guest at a wedding and you see this"
**Hook:** "POV: You're at a wedding. You see a sign that says 'Record a message for the bride.'"  
**Concept:** First-person perspective — walking up to the booth, seeing the phone, recording. Relatable, shareable, curiosity-driven.  
**CTA:** "Would you record a message? 👇 Comment below."

---

### 16. 🎬 "Things wedding guests actually remember vs. what hosts think they remember"
**Hook:** "Hosts spend $500 on centerpieces. Guests remember none of them."  
**Concept:** Trend-style list video — "Guests forget: the flowers ✗ · the tablecloths ✗ · the venue ✗. Guests remember: the moment they laughed, the speech that made everyone cry, the message they recorded for you ✓."  
**CTA:** "Give them something worth remembering. 🔗 Link in bio."

---

### 17. 🎬 "Rating wedding add-ons as a guest" (trend format)
**Hook:** "Rating wedding extras as someone who's been to 12 weddings this year."  
**Concept:** Comedic/relatable tier list — photo booth (meh) · open bar (S tier) · Love Booths ("wait, you get a video message from every guest?!" — S+ tier).  
**Tone:** Fun, light, shareable. Good for reach among younger audiences.

---

### 18. 🎬 "The part of the wedding nobody planned — and everyone loved"
**Hook:** "Nobody told the guests to line up. It just happened."  
**Concept:** Show guests organically forming a line to record their messages — laughter, anticipation, excitement. The unexpected hit of the event.  
**CTA:** "The best moments at your event aren't planned. Love Booths just makes sure they're captured."

---

## PROMOTIONAL / CONVERSION-FOCUSED

### 19. 🖼️ "Everything you get when you book Love Booths" (carousel)
**Hook slide:** "Here's exactly what happens when you book Love Booths for your wedding."  
**Slides:** Step-by-step breakdown of what the client receives — consultation, setup, the booth experience, video delivery, what the file looks like. Clear, visual, trust-building.  
**Final slide:** "Ready to book? DM us or click the link in bio."  
**Note:** This is a bottom-of-funnel piece. Run it as a retargeting ad.

---

### 20. 🎬 "Last-minute wedding add-ons that are actually worth it"
**Hook:** "If you're still finalizing your wedding vendors, add this one."  
**Concept:** Positioned as a helpful tip for brides still in planning mode — list format, Love Booths as the highlighted recommendation with a quick explanation of why.  
**CTA:** "Spots fill up fast for [season]. DM us your date."  
**Tone:** Helpful, not salesy. Works best as organic content with genuine value.
