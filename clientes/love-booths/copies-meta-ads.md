## A/B TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS

**Test 1 (Week 1–2):** Run Ad Set 1 Variant A vs. Variant B — cold audience, brides
- Win condition: lower CPL after 500+ impressions each

**Test 2 (Week 2–3):** Test top 2 winning headlines from Test 1 against 2 new headlines
- Win condition: CTR > 2% on the winning headline

**Test 3 (Week 3–4):** Add Ad Set 2 (quinceañera) — separate ad set, separate budget
- Win condition: CPL within 30% of bride campaign

**Scaling rule:** If ROAS > 2x or CPL is below target, increase budget by 20% every 4–5 days — never more than 30% at once to avoid resetting the algorithm's learning phase.
